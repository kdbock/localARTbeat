# ARTbeat Safety And Abuse Response Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026  
Last updated: March 26, 2026  
Owner: Local ARTbeat, LLC

This policy explains how ARTbeat handles abuse, threats, exploitation, and
other safety issues.

This document is operational guidance, not legal advice.

## Priority Safety Issues

ARTbeat prioritizes reports involving:

- minors safety
- child sexual abuse material or sexual exploitation
- threats of violence
- stalking or doxxing
- non-consensual imagery
- impersonation or fraud
- repeat harassment

## Response Targets

ARTbeat uses the following internal targets:

- urgent safety reports: initial review target within 24 hours
- standard abuse reports: initial review target within 72 hours

These are operational goals, not guaranteed deadlines.

## Investigation Authority

To investigate safety reports, ARTbeat may review:

- reported content
- reported accounts
- reported message threads
- attachments and directly related metadata
- prior moderation history tied to the same account or content

## Enforcement Actions

Depending on severity, ARTbeat may:

- remove content
- restrict features
- revoke access to abused sections of the platform
- block communication paths
- suspend accounts
- permanently terminate accounts
- preserve evidence for law enforcement or legal compliance

## Minors Safety

ARTbeat is not directed to children under 13.

Users under 18 may have restricted:

- messaging
- location sharing
- public profile discovery
- event participation

Any content involving sexual exploitation of minors, grooming, or pedophile
themes is prohibited and may be escalated immediately.

## Evidence Retention

ARTbeat may retain abuse reports, moderation notes, takedown records, and
related evidence for up to 7 years when needed for platform safety, dispute
handling, legal compliance, or enforcement continuity.
