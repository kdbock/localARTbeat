# Sponsorships

Sponsorships are the canonical paid local-business surface for Local ARTbeat.

Businesses submit sponsorships in the app, payment is captured through in-app purchase, and the request enters a review queue before it can appear in the product.

## Mobile Responsibilities

- show sponsorship purchase/submission screens
- collect business and creative details
- submit sponsorship records with `pending` status and IAP purchase metadata
- display approved/active sponsorships in supported placements

## Current Placements

- art walk header
- capture detail banner
- discovery/radar banner

## Out Of Mobile Scope

- approval queues
- moderation
- admin dashboards
- payout or financial operations

Those belong in the future private WordPress/plugin or backend moderator surface.
