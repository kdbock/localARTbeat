# ARTbeat Law Enforcement Request Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026  
Last updated: March 26, 2026  
Owner: Local ARTbeat, LLC

Law enforcement requests for ARTbeat should be sent to:

- Email: `kristy@kristykelly.com`
- Mail: `Local ARTbeat, LLC, PO BOX 232, Kinston, NC 28502`

## Handling Rule

All law enforcement requests should be routed through the owner contact above.

ARTbeat may request additional information to verify legal authority,
jurisdiction, scope, and urgency before responding.

## Emergency Requests

If a request involves imminent risk of death, serious bodily harm, or minors
safety, ARTbeat may prioritize review and preservation actions as quickly as
possible based on the information provided.

## Scope

ARTbeat may preserve or disclose information only where ARTbeat determines it is
legally required, reasonably necessary to respond to a valid request, or needed
to protect users or the platform from imminent harm.

This document is operational guidance, not legal advice.
