# ARTbeat Current Features

Last verified against routing and package structure: April 7, 2026

## Purpose

Provide a single, current list of live product capabilities by domain.

## Account And Identity

- Email/password authentication
- Social auth support (Google, Apple)
- User onboarding flow
- Profile creation/editing and profile media
- Favorites and profile deep-link routes

## Artist Platform

- Artist dashboard
- Multi-step artist onboarding flow
- Public artist profiles
- Artist analytics
- Artist artwork and feed views
- Artist earnings and payout request/account screens
- Artist approved-ads view

## Artwork

- Upload flows (choice, visual upload, written upload)
- Browse, featured, recent, trending, and search routes
- Artwork detail and edit
- Artwork auction setup and management
- Artwork purchase flow

## Capture

- Camera and creation routes
- Capture dashboard and browse/map routes
- Capture detail/edit/review routes
- Nearby/popular/my/pending/approved/public capture slices
- Capture admin moderation routes
- Capture terms/settings routes

## Art Walk

- Art walk dashboard/map/list/detail
- Art walk create/start/explore/search/nearby/popular
- Saved/completed/my-walks/my-captures
- Art walk achievements and settings
- Art walk moderation route
- Instant discovery route

## Community

- Community dashboard/feed/posts/featured/trending
- Community artists, portfolios, and studios
- Community search and create-post routes
- Community moderation and sponsorship routes
- Community messaging entry routes

## Events

- Discover and dashboard routes
- Event creation flow
- Event search and calendar routes
- My events and tickets routes
- Event detail routes

## Messaging

- Messaging hub and inbox
- New-chat routes
- User chat and thread routes

## Monetization

- In-app purchases for subscriptions, boosts, and ads
- Subscription comparison and plans
- Payment methods/screen/refund routes
- Local ads create/management/statistics/payment
- Sponsorship create and domain-specific sponsorship routes

## Admin And Operations

- Admin users/moderation/messaging/ad management/coupons routes
- Moderation coverage across community, captures, art walk, and artwork surfaces

## Cross-Cutting

- Multi-language localization (EN, ES, FR, DE, PT, ZH, AR)
- Upload safety/moderation service in shared core services
- Global startup diagnostics and error-handling bootstrap path

## Notes

This document tracks production-visible capabilities only. Historical implementation plans and dated rollouts are archived under `docs/archive/`.
