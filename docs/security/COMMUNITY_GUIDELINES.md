# ARTbeat Community Guidelines

Effective date: March 26, 2026  
Last updated: March 26, 2026  
Owner: Local ARTbeat, LLC

These guidelines describe what is and is not allowed on ARTbeat. They are
intended to protect artists, audiences, businesses, and the platform.

This document is operational guidance, not legal advice.

## Core Principle

ARTbeat exists to support art, artists, discovery, community, and commerce.
Use of the platform must not put other people at risk, exploit them, or abuse
their trust.

## Allowed Content

- original artwork, artist profiles, portfolios, and creative process content
- event listings, local art discovery, and related community discussion
- artistic nudity when presented in a legitimate artistic context
- criticism, commentary, and disagreement that does not become harassment

## Prohibited Conduct

The following is prohibited:

- harassment, threats, hate speech, bullying, or intimidation
- stalking, doxxing, or publishing private information without consent
- impersonation or deceptive representation of identity or affiliation
- off-platform scam solicitation, fraud, phishing, or unauthorized advertising
- non-consensual sexual content or intimate imagery
- pornography
- child sexual abuse material or any sexualized content involving minors
- copyright infringement or uploading content without rights or permission
- unlawful content or content that promotes violence or exploitation
- attempts to evade moderation, bans, or platform restrictions

## Artistic Nudity

ARTbeat may allow artistic nudity.

The following rules apply:

- artistic nudity may be permitted in a legitimate artistic context
- pornography is not allowed
- sexual exploitation, fetishized depictions of minors, or any pedophile-related
  content is prohibited
- ARTbeat is recommended for users 18 and older because artistic nudity or
  mature artistic subject matter may appear on the platform

## Minors

Users under 18 are allowed only subject to ARTbeat restrictions.

ARTbeat may restrict or disable for minors:

- direct messaging
- location sharing
- public profile discovery
- event participation or event-related interactions

ARTbeat is not directed to children under 13.

## Messaging Safety

Private messages are not read by default. If a message thread is reported for
abuse, safety, fraud, harassment, or other policy violations, ARTbeat admins or
moderators may review the reported thread, attachments, and directly related
metadata as needed to investigate and enforce platform rules.

## Reporting

Users should report content, users, or message threads that violate these
guidelines.

ARTbeat may prioritize reports involving:

- threats of violence or self-harm
- minors safety
- sexual exploitation
- stalking or doxxing
- fraud or impersonation

## Enforcement

ARTbeat may take any of the following actions:

- remove content
- restrict features
- revoke access to abused sections of the platform
- reject ads, posts, uploads, or messages
- suspend accounts
- permanently terminate accounts
- preserve evidence for investigation, legal compliance, and platform safety

Severe or repeated violations may result in immediate permanent removal.

## Appeals

Users may appeal moderation actions by contacting `support@localartbeat.com`.

ARTbeat may deny appeals where necessary to protect users, preserve evidence, or
comply with legal obligations.
