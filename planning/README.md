# Local ARTBeat Planning Hub

This folder is the central planning home for Local ARTBeat.

Local ARTBeat is currently best understood as an Eastern North Carolina arts discovery product. The website, app, content automation, arts council relationships, and event/opportunity intake should all work together to help people find and use local art experiences without sending them back to Facebook as the main community layer.

## Core Documents

- [Business Plan](BUSINESS_PLAN.md): what Local ARTBeat is, who it serves, how it can make money, and what should be avoided.
- [Growth Plan](GROWTH_PLAN.md): active acquisition and retention plan for app usage.
- [Weekly Operating System](WEEKLY_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md): recurring work cadence so the plan turns into action.
- [Open Decisions](OPEN_DECISIONS.md): unresolved strategy and product questions.

## Current Strategic Bet

Use local arts content on `localartbeat.com` to attract the right people, then move them into the Local ARTBeat app for saves, reminders, art walks, organization follows, grant tracking, and local arts discovery.

External platforms are acquisition channels. Local ARTBeat should become the place where the relationship lives.
