# Local ARTBeat Growth Plan

## Growth Principle

Do not try to get "everyone" to download the app. Get the people who already care about local arts to use Local ARTBeat for a specific job.

External platforms can help people discover Local ARTBeat, but the app should keep the relationship through saves, reminders, follows, collections, and submissions.

## Core Growth Loop

1. Arts organization posts or submits an event, grant, class, exhibit, or opportunity.
2. Local ARTBeat turns it into useful website/app content.
3. Interested users save, follow, or request reminders in the app.
4. The app brings users back with reminders and new relevant updates.
5. Arts organizations see value and keep submitting.

## Acquisition Channels

### Website Search

Use `localartbeat.com` to capture high-intent searches:

- Kinston art events
- Greenville art events
- Wilson art events
- Goldsboro art events
- Eastern NC art classes
- NC artist grants
- art walks Eastern NC
- things to do in Eastern NC art

Every relevant post should include an app CTA:

> Find more local art events, grants, galleries, and creative stops in the Local ARTBeat app.

### Partner Outreach

Start with a focused partner list:

- Community Council for the Arts Kinston
- Wilson Arts
- Emerge Gallery and Pitt County Arts Council
- Arts Council of Wayne County
- Farmville Community Arts Council
- Duplin Arts Collective
- Smart Kinston
- NC Arts Council grant network

Ask for submissions, not vague support.

Pitch:

> Local ARTBeat is building a free discovery layer for Eastern NC arts events, classes, grants, exhibits, and creative places. Send us your events and opportunities so more people can find them outside the Facebook algorithm.

### Event And Opportunity Intake

Build or prioritize a simple submission path:

- event name
- organization
- city
- date and time
- link
- category
- image optional
- contact email

This page should feed both editorial review and app listing creation.

### In-App Retention

Prioritize actions that keep people inside Local ARTBeat:

- save event
- remind me
- follow organization
- save grant deadline
- add to my weekend plan
- start art walk
- collect places to visit

Avoid making public Facebook sharing the main success path.

## 30-Day Plan

### Week 1: Foundation

- Add app CTA block to art news posts.
- Create or improve "Submit Your Art Event" page.
- Create landing pages for Kinston, Greenville, and Wilson.
- Confirm app store listing screenshots explain the simple wedge.

### Week 2: Partner Seeding

- Contact 10 local arts organizations.
- Ask each for current events, grants, classes, or calls for artists.
- Add the best feeds/sources to the content monitoring system.
- Feature 3 partner items on the website.

### Week 3: App Habit

- Promote saves, reminders, follows, and art walks.
- Create a "This Week in Eastern NC Arts" content lane.
- Identify which posts drive app clicks.
- Add one simple in-app prompt that encourages saving/following.

### Week 4: Review And Double Down

- Review website traffic by topic/city.
- Review app actions, not just installs.
- Pick the top 2 cities or topics.
- Repeat what worked and pause what did not.

## Metrics

Weekly acquisition metrics:

- art post page views
- app CTA clicks
- app installs
- event submission page visits
- partner submissions

Weekly activation metrics:

- first app opens
- first event view
- first save
- first follow
- first art walk open/start
- first grant/opportunity save

Weekly retention metrics:

- returning users
- reminders created
- saved items revisited
- followed organization updates viewed

## Current Biggest Bet

Local arts organizations already have content but poor distribution. Local ARTBeat can win by becoming the organized, searchable, app-native home for that content.
