# Local ARTBeat Business Plan

## Plain-English Summary

Local ARTBeat helps people discover local art, artists, events, classes, grants, galleries, public art, and creative places across Eastern North Carolina.

The business is not "generic news" and not "another social network." The business is a local arts discovery and participation platform.

## Problem

Local arts information is scattered across Facebook pages, outdated websites, flyers, email lists, and word of mouth. People miss events, artists miss opportunities, and arts organizations repeatedly post into platforms they do not control.

## Audience

Primary users:

- local artists
- arts councils and nonprofits
- galleries and creative venues
- parents looking for classes and camps
- residents looking for things to do
- visitors and day-trippers
- grant seekers and creative entrepreneurs

Secondary users:

- sponsors
- tourism groups
- municipalities
- schools and colleges
- downtown development groups
- local businesses tied to arts tourism

## Positioning

Local ARTBeat is your guide to local art, artists, events, grants, galleries, and creative places across Eastern North Carolina.

## Product Wedge

The first strong wedge is not the whole app ecosystem. It is:

- art events near me
- local art classes and camps
- NC artist grants and opportunities
- public art and art walks
- local galleries, venues, and arts organizations

## Why People Will Use It

Users get value when they can:

- find something to do this weekend
- save an event or class
- track an artist opportunity or grant deadline
- follow a local arts organization
- discover an art walk or creative place nearby
- submit an event without depending on Facebook reach

## Revenue Paths

Start with revenue that fits the community and does not damage trust.

1. Sponsored local arts guides
   - Example: "Kinston Arts Weekend Guide presented by..."
   - Best buyers: tourism groups, downtown organizations, local businesses, banks, chambers.

2. Featured organization profiles
   - Enhanced pages for galleries, arts councils, studios, and venues.
   - Keep basic listings free.

3. Event promotion
   - Paid boosts inside Local ARTBeat and on the website.
   - Clear labeling required.

4. Artist and organization tools
   - Paid tiers later for analytics, expanded profiles, portfolio tools, and promotion.

5. Grants and opportunities newsletter sponsorship
   - Strong fit because artists return for deadlines.

## What Not To Do Yet

- Do not make Local ARTBeat a broad news site.
- Do not chase every city at once.
- Do not force users into a complex social network before they get value.
- Do not send community behavior back to Facebook as the default.
- Do not build paid subscriptions before there is repeated free usage.

## Current Success Definition

Local ARTBeat is working when people use it to find, save, submit, or attend real arts events and opportunities in Eastern North Carolina.

## North Star Metric

Weekly active arts actions:

- event saves
- art walk starts
- organization follows
- grant/opportunity saves
- event submissions
- reminder signups
