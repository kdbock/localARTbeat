# Open Decisions

Use this file to hold decisions until there is enough evidence to choose.

## Product

- What is the simplest first-session experience for a brand-new user?
- Should browsing nearby events/art walks work before account creation?
- What app action matters most: save, follow, reminder, or art walk start?
- Should grant/opportunity tracking be a first-class feature?

## Website

- Which city landing pages should come first after Kinston, Greenville, and Wilson?
- Should `localartbeat.com/news/` remain news-only, or should guides and opportunities have separate sections?
- Where should app CTAs appear in WordPress posts?

## Partners

- What does an arts council profile need to be useful?
- Should organizations be able to submit events directly into the app later?
- What information should be required before an event can appear publicly?

## Revenue

- What should stay free forever?
- Which sponsorship product is easiest to sell first?
- Should promoted events exist, and how should they be labeled?
- What would an arts organization pay for only after free usage is proven?

## Scope

- Which broad ARTbeat features should be hidden or deemphasized until the core discovery loop is working?
- What belongs in Local ARTBeat versus future Scoopish?
