# Weekly Operating System

## Purpose

This is the repeatable weekly rhythm for turning Local ARTBeat from a built product into a used product.

## Monday: Measure

Review:

- website art traffic
- top posts by clicks
- app CTA clicks
- app installs
- first meaningful app actions
- event/opportunity submissions
- partner responses

Write down:

- what worked
- what did not
- one thing to repeat
- one thing to stop

## Tuesday: Source

Check:

- arts council feeds
- RSS.app social feeds
- NC Arts Council grants
- local galleries and venues
- school/college arts departments
- public art and tourism sources

Add promising items to the site/app pipeline.

## Wednesday: Partner Outreach

Contact 3 to 5 organizations.

Ask for one concrete thing:

- upcoming events
- classes
- grant deadlines
- calls for artists
- public art updates
- organization profile details

## Thursday: Product Improvement

Ship one small improvement tied to usage:

- clearer app CTA
- better submit form
- save/reminder/follow improvement
- landing page update
- app store screenshot/copy improvement
- analytics event verification

## Friday: Publish And Package

Create or polish:

- weekly arts guide
- grant/opportunity roundup
- city arts page update
- partner spotlight
- app listing candidate batch

## Weekend: Observe

Watch what real people click, save, submit, or ignore.

Do not overbuild from vibes alone. Let behavior steer the next week.
