## What Are Boosts?

Boosts are digital platform items that amplify artists' visibility and discovery momentum across ArtBeat. When you send a Boost, you activate immediate in-app effects that increase an artist's presence in discovery features, search results, and community engagement.

Boosts are designed to help artists gain traction within the platform while giving you visible recognition for your participation in the community.

## Why Send a Boost?

### For Users (You)

- **Amplify Discovery**: Your Boost directly increases an artist's visibility in search and recommendations
- **Boost Badges**: Earn visible recognition on artist profiles based on your engagement tier
- **Early Access**: Certain tiers unlock early access to new artwork releases
- **Engagement Trail**: Your profile appears in the artist's booster community
- **Platform Effects**: Watch your Boost activate real-time visibility changes

### For Artists

- **Discovery Visibility**: Boosted artists rank higher in search and discovery feeds
- **Momentum Scoring**: Boost activity contributes to the artist's discovery momentum
- **Profile Enhancement**: Visual effects like glowing map pins and featured placements
- **Priority Features**: Higher tiers trigger scheduled placement in key discovery areas
- **Community Signal**: Public booster trails show growing audience engagement
- **Platform Earnings**: Boost activity contributes to artist earnings through ArtBeat's creator revenue sharing program

## Artist Earnings & Revenue Sharing

**How It Works**:

Boosts are digital platform items. When you purchase a Boost:

1. The platform processes the in-app purchase through Apple/Google
2. Artist participation is recorded and tracked
3. Artists accumulate an **earnings balance** through ArtBeat's creator revenue sharing program
4. Earnings are tracked in the artist's earnings dashboard under "Boost Earnings"
5. Artists can request payouts once minimum thresholds are met
6. Payouts are processed off-platform through the creator program

**Key Points**:
- Artists view earnings in their ArtBeat dashboard
- Revenue is shared through the platform's creator program
- Minimum payout thresholds apply
- Processing time varies based on payment method
- All transactions comply with platform policies

**Note**: Boosts are non-refundable once purchased, as in-app effects occur immediately.

FAQ

Q: Can I boost myself?
A: No. Boosts are designed to highlight other artists in the community.

Q: Do boosts expire?
A: Momentum effects naturally decay over time, but badges and recognition remain.

Q: Can I send multiple boosts to the same artist?
A: Yes. Boost effects stack and apply immediately.

Q: What if an artist leaves ArtBeat?
A: Boost effects apply during their time on the platform and are not reversible.

Q: Can artists see who boosted them?
A: Yes. Supporter names and tiers are visible on artist profiles.

**Q: How are boosts different from donations?**  
A: Boosts are digital platform items that trigger in-app visibility effects. They're not direct financial transfers but contribute to artist earnings through the platform's revenue sharing program.

**Q: When do artists receive earnings?**  
A: Artists accumulate earnings in their platform balance and can request payouts once minimum thresholds are met. Processing occurs through the creator program.
